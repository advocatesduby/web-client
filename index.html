ABOUT US

Hanson Duby Lawyers

Being injured in an accident can be overwhelming. It can affect your health, your income, your family – your whole quality of life. That’s why you need to understand your rights – so you can get the compensation and treatment you deserve. We are available 24 hours a day in multiple languages including Tamil, Mandarin, Cantonese, Korean, Greek, and Assyrian and have a proven track record for winning some of the highest settlements for our clients. We will focus on you, so you can focus on your complete recovery.

Our job is to tell the story of what you have lost due to your accident, injury or illness. We will provide you with legal expertise to help you through the uncertainties you are feeling, and secure your future. You can also get support from our team of case managers, physiotherapists and occupational therapists. Our lawyers and staff are personable and approachable and we understand that being involved in a lawsuit can be stressful. We strive to ensure you are not overwhelmed by legal terms and procedure. When you retain our firm, you will also have substantial direct contact with our lawyers.

While waiting for your personal injury claim to settle, you will see a number of doctors, physiotherapists and other specialists. We work with them to develop the story of how your life has changed. Sometimes it takes the passage of time to fully appreciate what you have lost. After you retain us, we will deal directly with the insurance company on your behalf. We will respond to any inquiries and provide them with evidence of the extent of your injuries. We will keep you informed, but you do not have to worry about answering directly to an insurance company.
